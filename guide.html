Beginner's Guide
How to Play?
1
How to Register & Login?
Welcome to Raja Luck VIP! Joining is very simple:
  • Go to the Registration page.
  • Enter your valid mobile number.
  • Create a strong login password and remember it safely.
  • Enter the Invite Code (if any) to get special bonuses.
  • Click Register. Now you can easily login using your number and password.
2
How to Deposit / Recharge?
To start playing, you need to add funds to your wallet:
  • Go to the Wallet or Deposit section.
  • Select the amount you want to recharge (Min: ₹100).
  • Choose a payment method (UPI, Paytm, PhonePe, etc.).
  • Copy our UPI ID and make the payment from your app.
  • IMPORTANT: Copy the 12-digit UTR/Ref No. from your payment app and paste it in the box.
  • Your balance will be updated instantly!
3
How to Play WIN GO (Colors)?
Win Go is a 30-second color prediction game. It's very easy:
  • Colors: Bet on Green or Red. If you win, you get 2X (Double) your money.
  • Purple: If Purple comes with Red/Green, you get 1.5X. If you bet on Purple directly, you get 4.5X.
  • Numbers (0-9): Bet on a specific number. If your number comes, you win 9X (9 times your money!).
  • Big/Small: Numbers 0-4 are Small, 5-9 are Big. Winning gives 2X.
*Note: A 2% service fee is deducted on every bet.
4
How to Withdraw Money?
Won money? Time to withdraw it to your bank:
  • Go to the Withdraw section.
  • First, bind your Bank Account or UPI ID correctly.
  • Ensure you have completed the 3X Wagering requirement (Check status in withdraw page).
  • Enter the amount and your login password.
  • Click Withdraw. Funds usually arrive within a few minutes to 24 hours.
