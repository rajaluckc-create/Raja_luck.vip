Invitation rules
01
Inviting friends is divided into 6 levels. If A invites B, then B is A's level 1 friend. If B invites C, then C belongs to B's level 1 friend and also belongs to A's level 2 friend. If C invites D, then D belongs to C's first-degree friend, and also belongs to B's second-degree friend, and is A's third-degree friend.

02
When promoting a friend to sign up, you need to pass the link provided by the inviter or manually enter the inviter's invite code to become a first-level friend of the inviter.
03
Invited persons register via the invitation code and complete the top-up before the rebate incentive can take effect.
